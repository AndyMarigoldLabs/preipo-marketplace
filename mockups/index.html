HOW TRUST WORKS HERE

Both sides of the table prove what they claim before anyone talks.

Sellers prove shares

1
Document hash
Upload stock certificate or cap table entry. SHA-256 hash goes on-chain. Document stays with you.
2
Transfer agent verification
Third-party agent confirms you hold what you claim. Verifier address recorded on-chain.
3
Escrow attestation
Shares held in escrow. Escrow agent signs attestation recorded to the ListingRegistry contract.

Buyers prove funds

1
Stablecoin wallet
USDC/USDT/PYUSD balance checked on-chain. Tiered: <$25K, $25K–$250K, $250K–$1M, $1M+.
2
Bank verification
Plaid-verified bank balance. Account read-only — no transactions. Proof expires after 30 days.
3
Letter of credit
Upload institution LOC. Document hashed, stored on IPFS, hash recorded to chain. 30-day expiry.

What this is

A bulletin board where verified holders list pre-IPO positions they want to sell

Cryptographic proof that a seller owns shares and a buyer has capital — before anyone enters a deal room

Private deal rooms for document exchange and signing a deal memo between two parties

Not custodial. No assets, funds, or securities are held by the platform at any time.
